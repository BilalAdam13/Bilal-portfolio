About

01

My path into computer science started at Irvine Valley College and continued at USD, where I picked the Data Science and AI concentration because I wanted to work on systems that actually figure things out — not just store and display data. Most of what I've built since lives at that intersection: machine learning pipelines, retrieval-augmented generation systems, AI agents that watch markets, and graph algorithms operating on real-world datasets.

This past year I worked as an AI Extern at Wayfair building automated market-trend detection agents in n8n, and earlier at Outamation building NLP and OCR pipelines for mortgage document processing with LlamaIndex and RAG. My senior capstone is an iOS obstacle-avoidance app for blind users — Swift, CoreML, ARKit, and a custom Roboflow vision model.

Outside of school I play basketball, lift, and DJ. I like building things that take a real input, do something interesting with it, and produce an output you can show someone.
